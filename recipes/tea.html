Tea Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Water
  • 2 tsp Tea leaves
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 tsp Sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1-inch piece of Ginger (optional)
  • 2-3 Cardamom pods (optional)

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. If using ginger and cardamom, crush them slightly and add to the boiling water.
  3. Add 2 tsp of tea leaves to the boiling water. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add 1 cup of milk to the saucepan. Increase the heat and bring the mixture to a boil again.
  5. Add sugar to taste and stir well.
  6. Let the tea simmer for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend.
  7. Strain the tea into cups using a fine mesh strainer to remove the tea leaves and any ginger or cardamom pieces.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade tea!
